Our Services

SOURCING

MBG and Penumbra Minerals engage with reliable and high end quality suppliers. Bridging the gap between small to medium and the premium suppliers and processors of iron ore, manganese, and coal. Clients enjoy the benefits of already “ready to load” product at exit ports.

PROCUREMENT CONSULTANCY

We offer our clients comprehensive Procurement Consultancy.As your business partner, we are focussed on helping improve profitability. We cover the following areas: Cost management, Operational audits,Business improvement, General Procurement Training and more.

OUTSOURCE PURCHASE MANAGER

We provide a dedicated Outsourced Purchase Manager whose responsibility will be to ensure your procurements are being conducted efficiently and cost-effectively. A dedicated manager also gives you a single point of contact who will really understand what happens in your business.

PURCHASING

Negotiating the payment terms between buyers and suppliers. We strive to provide a both “win win” scenario where both buyer and supplier are not over exposed. Secure financial platform without hindering exchange of ownership of goods.

PURCHASE LEDGER ANALYSIS

We can provide a detailed analysis of your purchase ledger to identify where the potential cost-savings can be found. We also help you identify any losses that you may have incurred in your purchases, and also see where you may have been over-priced or over-charged by your suppliers.

PRICE NEGIOTIATION AND MANAGEMENT

We can negotiate price on your behalf. We need to know your budget and the highest price you are willing to pay then we can negotiate the best price possible from the suppliers. Price negotiation may be daunting for your staff if they don’t have experience doing so. We have been negotiating for over 20 years.

LOGISTICS AND CUSTOMS

We know that 50% of most companies’s carbon emissions are from logistics of goods. We work with various logistics partners who are local to your goods to ensure the lowest possible carbon footprint. We also work with customs clearing agents to assist you import your goods.
